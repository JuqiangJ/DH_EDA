Chapter 3 What is EDA?

3.1 Data analysis workflow

A common workflow for data analysis involves importing data, cleaning data, transforming data, visualizing and modeling data for reports or papers. However, you may notice that it is not a linear process. In other words, there is magic way of understanding your data with a touch. Rather, it is a process where you need to try different wayd of tranforming, visualizing and modeling your data. Therefore, EDA is not a formal process with a strict set of rules, but an open process.

Luckily, R offers a range of tools that can help us eaily summarize, visualize and model our data.

In this workshop, we first explore variation within a variable and then move on to covariation among variables.
